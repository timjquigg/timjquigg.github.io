About Me

I am a Professional Engineer from Calgary with 8 years of experience in the oil and gas sector and more than 4 years in the automotive service section starting out my journey as a web developer.

I always enjoyed learning computer programming in highschool and university but never thought about making it a career until the recent declines in traditional engineering jobs in Calgary in oil and gas. In highschool I recieved an introduction to writing programs in Java. In university I took a course in the C programming language and used those skills to perform calculations throughout my university program.

Once I entered the engineering workforce, my computer programming sat mostly on the back burner. I occasionally wrote automations in Microsoft Excel using Visual Basic. But for the most part, my coding muscles sat dormant.

The lagging oil and gas industries in Calgary have really made me re-examine what I want to be doing and as my wife asked me last year "what I wanted to do when I grow up." Funny to think of being asked that question already 10+ years into the workforce. But it reminded me of the excitement I always experienced when designing an algorithm or the joy I recieved when I finally figured out a bug and my code gave finally me the results I was expecting.

So better late than never, I'm honing my coding skills and looking to transition to a career in development!
